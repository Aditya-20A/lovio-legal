Privacy Policy for Lovio
Last Updated: June 22, 2026

At Lovio ("we," "our," "us"), we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. Lovio is a private space designed exclusively for couples to stay connected, chat, and share locations. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. 1. NO COMMERCIALIZATION OF USER DATA We do not sell, rent, trade, or commercialize your personal information, chats, notes, memories, or location logs to third parties, advertisers, or data brokers. All data collected is strictly used to power the application's couple-sharing features. 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND HOW WE USE IT • Authentication: We use Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In to secure your account. We collect your email address, display name, and a unique identifier (UID). • Location Sharing: To power the real-time couple map and distance features, Lovio collects precise and approximate location data, including background location data (even when the app is closed or not in use), via a foreground notification service. This location data is shared only with your paired partner. • Private Chat: Text and compressed image messages are securely transmitted and stored. Chat data is accessible only by you and your paired partner. • Memories and Photos: Photos uploaded to your memories album are stored in secure buckets on Supabase and shared exclusively with your partner. • Shared Notes: Notes and lists created inside the app are stored in our secure database for sync. • Push Notifications: We collect Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens to route notifications when your partner sends a message or updates location. 3. DATA RETENTION AND ACCOUNT DELETION • Location Logs: We only retain location logs needed to calculate distance and show active location. Old location coordinates are automatically pruned. • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time in-app (Profile -> Account -> Delete Account) or via our web form. Upon deletion, your profile, couple connection, chat messages, notes, and Supabase storage photo folders are permanently erased from our servers within 30 days. 4. GDPR AND CCPA COMPLIANCE • CCPA: We do not sell your personal data. • GDPR: EU users have the right to access their data, request a copy, or request erasure ("right to be forgotten"). Please contact us at support@love8.com to exercise these rights. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@love8.com.
