Delete Your Lovio Account

You can delete your account instantly inside the Lovio app by navigating to Profile > Account Settings > Delete Account. If you no longer have access to the app, you can submit a manual deletion request below.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data is removed upon account deletion?

We permanently delete your user profile, connection link with your partner, all private chat messages, shared notes, and Supabase storage folders containing your uploaded photos and thumbnails.

How long does the deletion process take?

In-app account deletions are executed instantly. Manual requests submitted via this web form are processed and completed within 7 business days.

Can I cancel a deletion request?

No. Once the deletion is processed, it is permanent and all data is unrecoverable.
