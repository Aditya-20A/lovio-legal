Lovio Child Safety Standards

Lovio is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, or any activity that exploits minors.

Our Standards

  • Users may not upload, share, request, or distribute child sexual abuse material.
  • Any attempt to exploit or groom minors is strictly prohibited.
  • Accounts violating these standards will be permanently suspended.
  • Illegal content may be reported to appropriate authorities where required by law.
  • Users can report abusive behavior directly through our support contact.

Reporting Abuse

If you believe a user is violating these standards, please contact:

Email: support@lovio.app

Age Requirement

Lovio is intended for users who meet the minimum age requirements applicable in their region. Children under the permitted age should not use the service.

Law Enforcement Cooperation

Lovio cooperates with lawful requests from authorities involving child safety investigations.
